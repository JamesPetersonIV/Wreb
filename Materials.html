Materials

At WREB we pride ourselves in reducing plastic and resource use.
How? You may ask. We limit ourselves to two materials in the production of our product.

Straw

The straw is used as support for your heavier items. Thin straw is used to make the WREB more flexible for folding purposes.

Burlap

The burlap's purpose is mainly for style, but we also use it to secure the inside of the bag.
